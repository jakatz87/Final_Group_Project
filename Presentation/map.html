TABLEAU VISUALS

We are excited to offer you a variety of interactive graphs to explore. With just a simple hover of your mouse, you can interact with the graphs and discover new insights. Our interactive graphs are designed to be user-friendly, so you can easily navigate and explore the data. Don't be afraid to hover over different areas to see what information is available to you. We hope you enjoy using our interactive graphs and that they help you gain a better understanding of the data presented.

AVERAGE TEMPERATURE BY STATE

This map of Average Temperature by State is an essential tool for those considering vacation property investment risk.
Correlating this information to the location and activities they know tourists want helps inform smarter decisions!

INBOUND TRAVELERS BY STATE

An Inbound Travelers by State map helps in understanding how many people travel to a specific destination along with the existing population
of that destination which can help inform the investor where people tend to vacation and how rentable a property may be.

REAL ESTATE BUYERS BY STATE

This graph represents the percentage of real estate buyers by state. This graph will help inform an vacation property investor which state has the highest
percentage of home buyers which will enable the investor to choose a state that will likely have a high inventory of properties to purchase from.

US QUALITY OF LIFE

Quality of Life is essential. Factors such as cost of living, healthcare, pollution and safety all play apart in a person's quality of life.
This graph easily shows an investor which state or states has the optimum quality of life based on their needs.

MEDIAN GOVERNMENT SPENDING

In this visualization an investor can identify how much the government spends on the outdoors. This would be an indicator of the amount and type outdoor
activities would be available to a potential renter, such as bicycling, boating, fishing, hunting, amusement/water parks, game areas, guided tours, etc.
